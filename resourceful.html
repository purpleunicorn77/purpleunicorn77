ðŸŒ¿ Conclusion ðŸŒ¿

Green is the elusive color is our topic. We were not picking a side of whether the green transition is good or not. We only hope the government, companies, and the indigenous people can find the balance. Renewable energy is a great intention to save our earth, but exploiting aboriginal livelihood is not the best solution. Moving onshore wind farms to offshore wind farms may be a better option. However, regulating the offshore route and policy is another problem. Humans are always creating problems and finding solutions. From this project, we were also speaking up for people with fewer resources.
